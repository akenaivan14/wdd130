AKENA WHITEWATER TRAVELERS

At Whitewater Travelers, our mission is to create unforgettable river experiences that connect people with nature while prioritizing safety and environmental stewardship. We believe that the rush of navigating rapids builds confidence, teamwork, and lasting memories.

History

Founded in 2001 by river Ivan Akena and Maguire Johansen, Whitewater Adventures began with just two rafts and a passion for sharing the thrill of whitewater with others. What started as weekend excursions for friends has grown into one of the region's most respected rafting companies, now offering trips on five different rivers with a fleet of 15 rafts and a team of 12 certified guides.
